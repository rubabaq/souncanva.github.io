Clifton Art Gallery (Karachi, Pakistan)


Clifton Art Gallery, established in 1991, is one of Karachi's premier art galleries, dedicated to promoting Pakistani art and artists. The gallery showcases a diverse collection of artworks, including landscapes, abstracts, calligraphy, figurative paintings, and miniatures, featuring works by renowned Pakistani artists such as A.Q. Arif, A.S. Rind, Iqbal Hussain, Jamil Naqsh, and Sadequain. Clifton Art Gallery has hosted numerous exhibitions, including the last three exhibitions of the esteemed artist Colin David, attracting both students and art connoisseurs. KARACHI ART DIRECTORY The gallery is located at F-44/1, Block 4, Clifton (E-street), Karachi, Pakistan. Clifton Art Gallery continues to be a significant contributor to Pakistan's art scene, fostering appreciation for the country's rich cultural heritage and contemporary artistic endeavors.
