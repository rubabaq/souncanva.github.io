Banksy (Unknown, active since the 1990s)

Banksy is an anonymous English street artist known for his provocative and politically charged works. His graffiti art often includes dark humor and satirical social commentary. His works, such as Girl with Balloon and There Is Always Hope, have become iconic, with some pieces even selling for millions of dollars. Banksy's art frequently challenges the boundaries of art and society, and his identity remains a subject of intrigue.

Banksy’s Work

Girl With The Balloon

"Girl with a Balloon" by Banksy, created in 2002, encapsulates themes of innocence, loss, and hope. The artwork features a young girl reaching out for a red balloon, symbolizing dreams and desires. Banksy’s thought process likely reflects the fragility of life and how fleeting moments of happiness can slip away. The piece is often interpreted as a commentary on the innocence of childhood contrasted with the harsh realities of life. Its street art origins and spontaneous style embody Banksy's subversive critique of societal norms. The image became iconic, especially after a version self-destructed at auction in 2018.

Estimated Value: $25.4 million
Love Is In The Bin

Love is in the Bin is a provocative piece by street artist Banksy, created in 2018 when his famous artwork Girl with a Balloon was shredded moments after being sold at auction. The act was a statement on the commodification of art and its market value. Banksy, known for his anonymous activism, turned the destruction into a new work, highlighting the fleeting nature of art and challenging perceptions of value in a consumer-driven world. The stunt, blending shock with creativity, reinforced Banksy's critique of art institutions and the high-end art market.

The Flower Thrower

The Flower Thrower by Banksy, created in 2005, depicts a man in a throwing pose, but instead of a Molotov cocktail, he holds a bouquet of flowers. This piece critiques violence and war, symbolizing peace through the contrast of a traditionally violent gesture with a soft, non-threatening object. Banksy, a pseudonymous street artist from Bristol, England, often incorporates political themes in his work. His guerrilla art, typically created in public spaces, challenges societal norms and speaks to issues like war, consumerism, and inequality. The Flower Thrower exemplifies his belief in the power of love and peace to disrupt violence and chaos.

Estimated Value: $99.99
